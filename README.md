# SUPERSTORE-SALES-AND-PROFIT-ANALYSIS-DASHBOARD-2014---2017-
This project analyzes retail transaction data from a Superstore dataset to uncover insights into sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and product demand.

---

## 📁 Project Overview
This project analyzes retail transaction data from a Superstore dataset to uncover insights into **sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and product demand**. An interactive Power BI dashboard was developed to help stakeholders quickly monitor **business performance, identify profitable product categories, evaluate regional sales trends**, and support data-driven decision making.
The dataset contains 9,994 retail transactions with information on** orders, customers, products, discounts, sales, and profits**.

---

## 📊 Key Metrics (Overall)
| **Metric** | Value |
|---------|---------|
| **Total Sales** | $2,297,200 |
| **Total Profit** | $286,397 |
| **Total Orders** |  5,009 |
| **Total Quantity Sold** | 37,873 |
| **Average Order Value** | $458.61 |
| **Profit Margin** | 12.47% |

---

## ⚒️ Tools Used
- Power BI – Data visualization and dashboard development
-	Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation
-	DAX – Calculated measures and KPIs
-	Excel – Dataset storage and preprocessing

  ---

  ## 🧹 Data Preparaion Steps
- Imported the dataset from Excel into Power BI.
- Cleaned the data by checking for missing values and duplicate records.
- Verified and corrected data types for columns such as Sales, Profit, Quantity, and Dates.
- Standardized column names for better consistency and readability.
- Created a calculated field for Profit Margin (Profit ÷ Sales).
- Validated the dataset to ensure accurate calculations and analysis before building the dashboard.

  ---

 ## 🔍 Dashboard Insights
 - Technology is the highest revenue-generating category, contributing the largest share of total sales.
 -  The West region records the highest sales, making it the top-performing region for the business.
 - 	Phones and Chairs are the top-selling sub-categories, showing strong customer demand.
 -	The business maintains an overall profit margin of about 12%, indicating moderate profitability.
 - Some products generate high sales but lower profits, suggesting that discounts may be affecting profitability.
 - Office Supplies, Furniture, and Technology all contribute significantly to total sales, showing diversified revenue sources.

---

## Recommendations
- Increase focus on Technology products since they generate the highest sales and strong customer demand.
- Expand marketing and sales efforts in the West region to further capitalize on its strong performance.
- Promote high-performing sub-categories like Phones and Chairs through targeted campaigns and inventory optimization.
- Review discount strategies to ensure they do not significantly reduce profit margins.
- Improve profitability in lower-performing regions by adjusting pricing strategies or introducing targeted promotions.
- Monitor product performance regularly to identify items that generate high sales but low profits and adjust strategies accordingly.

  ---

  ## Conclusion
  The Superstore Sales and Profit Analysis dashboard provides valuable insights into the company’s sales performance, profitability, and product demand. The analysis shows that Technology products and the West region contribute the most to overall sales, while sub-categories like Phones and Chairs drive strong customer demand. Although the business maintains a moderate profit margin, reviewing discount strategies and improving performance in lower-performing regions could further increase profitability. Overall, the dashboard helps support data-driven decision-making by clearly highlighting key trends and opportunities for business growth.
